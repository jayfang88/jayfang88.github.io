Hi! I'm Justin

Soon after college graduation I began working in UX Research Operations, alongside UX Researchers and Designers. With increasing exposure to UX/UI Design in my workplace, I started tinkering with frontend development and design myself and immediately knew I wanted to educate myself further with web development.

I am currently a San Francisco-based software engineer who loves tackling complex problems and learning about logic. As a developer, I am experienced with JavaScript, React, Redux, Node.js, Express, Ruby / Rails, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, HTML5, CSS3, and Git. I strive to use not just my skills as a software developer but also my background in UX Research and Psychology to build dynamic web applications and seamless user interfaces.
